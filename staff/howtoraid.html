Server Classification

As you select each option, add up the marks and classify the server at the bottom of the list

  1. How much effort does it take to raid the server?

    1. A shit ton of effort - 1 point
    2. A lot of effort - 2 points
    3. A moderate amount of effort - 3 points
    4. A little bit of effort - 4 points
    5. No effort - 5 points

  2. How many people are affected by this raid in the server?

    1. 0 - 0 points
    2. 0 to 20 - 1 point
    3. 21 to 40 - 2 points
    4. 41 to 100 - 3 points
    5. 101 to 500 - 4 points
    6. 501+ - 5 points

  3. How well do dupes work?

    1. There are no dupes - 0 points
    2. The dupes are very inefficient (take a long time and dupe a few amount of items at a time) - 1 point
    3. The dupes are inefficient - 2 points
    4. The dupes are not efficient, but it is still worth duping - 3 points
    5. The dupes are efficient - 4 points
    6. The dupes are very efficient (takes little time to dupe and you can dupe the items you duped making the dupiing exponential) - 5 points
